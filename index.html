Our Story

FINO Tequila is a Women Owned, Chicago based brand, with a passion for providing extremely smooth, premium, sipping tequilas that can be enjoyed by both men and women. The taste and flavor is ensured from the outstanding quality, natural colors (without additives) and distinctive aromas from the finest agave azul, grown in the rich volcanic red soils in the highlands of Jalisco, Mexico. From place and production, to people and passion, Fino Tequila’s distillery has been a family business for more than three generations, defining mastery and commitment to quality of the product. The term FINO, in Spanish, means fine, smooth and suave. FINO is also an acronym for Failure Is Not an Option. The owners, Jana and Sonali, truly believe in this philosophy and this stems their exceptional dedication to their brand, FINO Tequila.
